Public Grievance Redressal Portal

Empowering Citizens to Report and Resolve Local Issues Transparently

About Us

The Public Grievance Redressal Portal is an initiative under the Digital India Mission to allow citizens to report public issues such as damaged roads, streetlight failures, water supply disruptions, sanitation problems, and more. Our goal is to ensure transparency, accountability, and timely response from government departments.

Register complaints related to power outages, faulty lines, or water supply problems.
