Issue Desk Of Government of India

Empowering Citizens to Report and Resolve Local Issues Transparently

About the Portal

The Issue Desk Of Government of India is a government initiative allowing citizens to report local issues such as damaged roads, water supply failures, streetlight outages, or sanitation problems. It ensures transparency, accountability, and a timely response from responsible authorities.
