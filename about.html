Located in Coimbatore, Sri Shakthi Institute is a premier engineering college that offers quality education with a focus on innovation, research, and holistic development.

About Us

Sri Shakthi Institute of Engineering and Technology (SIET) was established with the goal of producing industry-ready engineers. The college is affiliated to Anna University and approved by AICTE. With a mission to become a center of excellence, SIET provides top-notch infrastructure, experienced faculty, and an environment that nurtures learning and creativity.
